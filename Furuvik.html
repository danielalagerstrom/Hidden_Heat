Discover Furuvik's Rantasauna: Helsinki's year-round authentic sauna

Escape to Furuvik Rantasauna, a one-of-a-kind beach sauna experience in Helsinki. Open all year round, this sauna offers a refreshing and rejuvenating retreat with open sauna and swimming sessions available almost every day. Whether it's winter's chill or the warmth of summer, you can always take a plunge in the sea after your sauna session.

Tickets for Furuvik Rantasauna are available through the Saunat.fi ticket shop, and with a limited number of tickets per session, you’re guaranteed a peaceful and intimate experience. Swim in the sea any time of year, with easy access from a pier equipped with sturdy stairs. In the winter, an opening pump ensures the water remains accessible, no matter the season.

After your sauna, rinse off in the shower room, featuring two showers and a small changing room. For more space, head to the changing room trolley, which includes lockers to safely store your valuables while you unwind. Furuvik Rantasauna offers the perfect combination of Finnish sauna tradition and the refreshing beauty of the sea—an ideal spot to relax, recharge, and embrace the elements. Book your session today!
