Relax and Unwind at Allas Sea Pool: Sauna by the Sea

At Allas Sea Pool, indulge in a truly unique sauna experience with stunning sea views. With five saunas to choose from—three floating on the water and two located in the main building. You will be immersed in the ultimate combination of relaxation and breathtaking surroundings. As you bask in the warmth of the sauna, enjoy panoramic views of the sparkling open sea, making every visit a serene escape.

Allas Sea Pool offers something for everyone. Whether you're a swimmer, an exerciser, or just looking to relax, the heated pool and seawater pool cater to all your needs. The heated pool stays at a comfortable 27 degrees all year round, while the children’s pool is open during the summer season, making it a perfect spot for families. Whether you're a local or visiting from afar, Allas Sea Pool is the ideal place to unwind, refresh, and experience the beauty of Helsinki. Come whenever suits you, and soak in the sweetest moments of this iconic seaside retreat!

What makes it a hidden gem:

While it is a well-known spot among locals, it often flies under the radar for tourists, giving it that "hidden gem" status. It offers an intimate, authentic Finnish sauna experience that is a great alternative to the more crowded tourist attractions

Fun Fact:

TThe construction project of Allas Sea Pool was financed, among other things, by crowdfunding, which raised over 810,000 euros. Over 400 people participated in the crowdfunding. There is a "hall of fame" on the wall of Allas Sea Pool, where you can see the names of all the investors.
